2D&3D DESIGN

-QUEEN MOTHER OR CHESS PIECE

This project explores the metamorphosis of the Oriental Phoenix: a symbol of noble spiritual values, through a modern digital lens.

By merging intricate 2D illustrations with 3D glass-material modeling in Blender, I transform traditional heritage into a futuristic visual experience.

It is an endeavor to redefine Vietnamese tradition: not as a static relic, but as a living entity thriving in the digital age.
