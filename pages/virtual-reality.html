VIRTUAL REALITY

-ANGELESS MUSE

Ageless Muse is an immersive Virtual Reality (VR) exhibition dedicated to honoring the image of Vietnamese women through the ages, from the feudal period to the modern day.

By leveraging VR technology, the project creates a digital sanctuary where traditional beauty transcends time and space.

The exhibition aims to showcase the application of heritage values in modern design, proving that historical aesthetics are a vital source of inspiration and identity for contemporary branding.
