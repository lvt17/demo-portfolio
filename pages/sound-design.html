SOUND DESIGN

-THE BOOGEYMAN

This was a sound design project for a puzzle-horror game revolving around the journey of a boy named A Xin.

I was directly involved in building the sound world and designing the project documentation.

The main goal of the project was to use sound to celebrate the diversity of Vietnamese culture, from folk games and traditional music to mythological stories, in order to create a digital experience that is deeply spiritual and mysterious.
